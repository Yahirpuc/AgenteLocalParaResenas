Usuario:   Lancelot
Estrellas: 5★
Opinión:   A very good docking station - even for Mac, as long as you understand its limitations. I'm currently using this with a MacBook Pro and a single monitor. I have other peripherals plugged in - keyboard, mouse, audio interface, music keyboard. It provides a very elegant solution, one usb-c connection and the screen springs into life and all my peripherals with just one connection. It also charges my laptop and does a time machine back-up. A very simple solution to providing a pretty instant with between laptop and desktop. I've had one incident where it decided to switch on and off a couple of times, not sure why. But its functioned very well for pretty much the whole time I've had it and I'm very happy with the very easy solution it provides.

UPDATE - the incident above, switching on and off has happened again and I tracked it down to an issue with leaving hard drives connected to the docking station when the computer is unplugged- and later reconnected. You need to physically disconnect any hard drives from the dock when the computer isn't connected as they seem to confuse the computer (MacBook Pro) and cause the intermittent connection issue. This may be helpful to others who've had similar issues. The drives can quite happily be reconnected once the computer has been connected to the dock
